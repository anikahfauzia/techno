Introduction#

Are you looking for a delicious and moist vanilla cake recipe that can last for four days? Look no further! This recipe is sure to be a hit with your family and friends.

Overview of the Vanilla Cake#

This vanilla cake is a classic and simple recipe that can be adapted to fit any occasion. It’s made with simple ingredients and is sure to be a crowd pleaser. The cake is moist and fluffy, and can be decorated any way you like.

Benefits of the Vanilla Cake#

The best part about this cake is that it can last up to four days in the refrigerator. This means that you can make it ahead of time and not have to worry about it going bad. Plus, it’s a great way to save time and money.

Ingredients#

Here is a list of ingredients you will need for this recipe:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter (softened)
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk

Preparing the Ingredients#

Before you start baking, make sure all of your ingredients are ready to go. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.

Recipe#

Now it’s time to get baking!

Baking the Cake#

In a large bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. In a separate bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time. Add the vanilla extract and mix until combined.

Alternate adding the dry ingredients and the milk to the butter mixture. Mix until just combined. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and bake for 25-30 minutes. Let the cake cool before decorating.

Decorating the Cake#

Once the cake has cooled, you can decorate it any way you like. You can use frosting, sprinkles, or whatever you have on hand.

Storing the Cake#

Once the cake is decorated, you can store it in the refrigerator for up to four days. Just make sure to cover it with plastic wrap or aluminum foil.

Conclusion#
