A. Overview of Slow Cooked Beef Cheek Recipe#

The slow cooked beef cheek recipe is a simple yet delicious meal. It starts with selecting a quality beef cheek, which is a cut of meat from the cow’s face. This cut of meat is known for its tenderness and flavor. Once you have the beef cheek, you’ll need to prepare the ingredients, assemble the recipe, and then slow cook the beef cheek in a slow cooker.

B. Benefits of Slow Cooking#

Slow cooking has many benefits, including tenderizing the meat and locking in the flavor. Slow cooking also allows you to use less expensive cuts of meat, as it helps tenderize them. Additionally, slow cooking can save you time and energy, as you can set the slow cooker and forget about it until the meal is done.

II. Preparing the Beef Cheek#

A. Selecting the Beef Cheek#

When selecting the beef cheek, it’s important to choose a quality cut of meat. Look for a cut that is well marbled and has a nice fat content. If you’re having trouble finding a quality cut of beef cheek, ask your butcher for advice.

B. Preparing the Ingredients#

Once you have the beef cheek, it’s time to prepare the ingredients. You’ll need garlic, onion, carrots, celery, beef stock, tomato paste, and red wine. Chop the vegetables and mix them with the beef stock and tomato paste.

C. Assembling the Recipe#

Once the ingredients are prepared, it’s time to assemble the recipe. Place the beef cheek in the slow cooker and top it with the vegetables and the beef stock mixture. Pour the red wine over the top and set the slow cooker to low.

III. Cooking the Beef Cheek#

A. Setting the Slow Cooker#

Set the slow cooker to low and cook the beef cheek for 8-10 hours. This will allow the beef cheek to become tender and flavorful.

B. Slow Cooking the Beef Cheek#

Once the beef cheek is in the slow cooker, all you have to do is wait! The slow cooking process will tenderize the meat and lock in the flavor.

C. Finishing the Dish#

Once the beef cheek is cooked, it’s time to finish the dish. Remove the beef cheek from the slow cooker and shred it with two forks. Place the shredded beef cheek back into the slow cooker and stir in any remaining liquid.

D. Serving the Slow Cooked Beef Cheek#

Now that the slow cooked beef cheek is ready, it’s time to serve it! Serve the beef cheek with mashed potatoes, steamed vegetables, or your favorite sides. Enjoy!

Preparation#

Ingredients Needed#

  • 2 lbs beef cheeks
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions#

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

  2. In a large oven-safe Dutch oven, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat.

  3. Add the beef cheeks and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.

  4. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 3 minutes.

  5. Add the red wine and beef broth and bring to a simmer.

  6. Add the thyme, oregano, rosemary, salt and pepper and stir to combine.

  7. Cover the Dutch oven and transfer to the oven.

  8. Cook for 2-3 hours, or until the beef cheeks are tender.

  9. Serve with mashed potatoes or a side of vegetables.
