This Fish Cake Recipe Will Make You Never Want to Eat Anything Else!#

Fish cakes are a delicious and nutritious meal that can be enjoyed by everyone. They are easy to make and can be served as a main dish or as an appetizer. This recipe is sure to make you never want to eat anything else!

Overview of Fish Cake Recipe#

This fish cake recipe is simple and flavorful. It requires few ingredients and can be cooked in under 30 minutes. The main ingredients are fish fillets, bread crumbs, eggs, and a few other seasonings. The fish is mixed with the other ingredients, formed into patties, and then fried in a pan. The result is a delicious, crispy fish cake that will have your taste buds singing!

Benefits of Eating Fish Cakes#

Fish cakes are a great source of protein and essential vitamins and minerals. They are also low in fat and calories, making them a healthy and satisfying meal. Eating fish cakes can help to reduce the risk of heart disease, stroke, and other chronic diseases. Additionally, fish cakes are a great way to get your daily dose of omega-3 fatty acids, which are essential for good health.

Reasons to Try This Recipe#

This fish cake recipe is easy to make and you can customize it to your own tastes. You can experiment with different types of fish, seasonings, and other ingredients. You can also make the fish cakes in advance and store them in the refrigerator for up to three days.

Ingredients#

  • 2 pounds of fish fillets (any type of fish can be used)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of bread crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Substitutions for Certain Ingredients#

If you don’t have bread crumbs, you can use crushed crackers or cornflakes as a substitute. You can also use fresh herbs instead of dried herbs. If you don’t have olive oil, you can use vegetable oil or butter.

Tips on Selecting the Right Fish#

When selecting the fish for your fish cakes, it is important to choose a type of fish that is fresh and not overly fishy. You can ask your fishmonger for advice on which type of fish is best for your recipe.

Preparation#

Step-by-Step Instructions#

  1. Begin by preparing the fish. Rinse the fish fillets and pat dry with a paper towel.

  2. Place the fish in a bowl and add the eggs, bread crumbs, olive oil, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Mix the ingredients together until they are well combined.

  3. Form the mixture into patties, using your hands.

  4. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add a few tablespoons of oil.

  5. Place the fish cakes in the skillet and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.

Tips on Cooking Fish Cakes#

  • Make sure the skillet is hot before adding the fish cakes.
  • Be careful not to overcrowd the skillet.
  • Flip the fish cakes gently to avoid breaking them.

Suggested Serving Options#

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) :#

Q1: What ingredients do you need to make this fish cake recipe?

A1: You will need fish fillets, eggs, breadcrumbs, butter, onion, garlic, parsley, and salt and pepper.

Q2: How do you prepare the fish for the recipe?

A2: First, you need to rinse the fish fillets in cold water and pat them dry. Then, cut the fillets into small cubes and season with salt and pepper.

Q3: How do you make the fish cake mixture?

A3: In a bowl, mix together the fish cubes, eggs, breadcrumbs, butter, onion, garlic, and parsley. Use your hands to mix everything together until it is well combined.

Q4: How do you shape the fish cakes?

A4: Take a handful of the fish cake mixture and shape it into a patty. Place the patty on a plate and repeat the process until all of the mixture is gone.

Q5: How do you cook the fish cakes?

A5: Heat a pan over medium-high heat and add some oil. Place the fish cakes in the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown. Serve with your favorite sides and enjoy!
