Overview of Broccoli Rice and Chicken Casserole#

Recipe#

Ingredients#

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, cubed
  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 2 cups broccoli, steamed and chopped
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions#

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooked chicken, rice, broccoli, cream of chicken soup, and salt and pepper.
  3. Spread mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. Sprinkle cheese and breadcrumbs over the top.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
