Introduction#

What is Chinese Beef and Broccoli?#

Chinese Beef and Broccoli is a classic Chinese dish that is made with beef, broccoli, and a delicious savory sauce. The beef is usually marinated and stir-fried with the broccoli, and the sauce is usually a combination of soy sauce, garlic, and ginger. The end result is a savory and flavorful dish that is sure to please any palate.

Benefits of Preparing This Recipe#

There are many benefits to preparing this recipe. First, it is a quick and easy meal that can be prepared in just a few minutes. Second, it is a healthy dish that is packed with protein, vitamins, and minerals. Third, it is a versatile dish that can be served as an entrée or as a side dish. Finally, it is a delicious dish that is sure to please the whole family.

Ingredients#

For this recipe, you will need the following ingredients:

  • Beef – 1 pound of flank steak, thinly sliced
  • Broccoli – 2 heads of broccoli, cut into florets
  • Sauce – 1/4 cup of soy sauce, 2 cloves of minced garlic, and 1 teaspoon of freshly grated ginger

Preparation#

The first step in preparing this dish is to prepare the beef. Place the thinly sliced flank steak in a bowl and season with salt and pepper. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the beef and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the beef is browned and cooked through. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.

Next, prepare the broccoli. In the same skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium-high heat. Add the broccoli florets and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the broccoli is tender. Remove the broccoli from the skillet and set aside.

Now, make the sauce. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, garlic, and ginger.

Finally, combine and cook the ingredients. Return the beef and broccoli to the skillet and pour the sauce over the top. Stir to combine and cook for 2-3 minutes, until the sauce is thick and bubbly.

Serving#

When it comes to serving this dish, presentation is key. To serve, arrange the beef and broccoli on a platter and drizzle the sauce over the top.

This dish can be served as an entrée or as a side dish. If you are serving it as an entrée, accompany it with steamed white rice or fried rice. If you are serving it as a side dish, pair it with your favorite Chinese dishes.
