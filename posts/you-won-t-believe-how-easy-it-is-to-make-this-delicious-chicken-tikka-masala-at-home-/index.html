Introduction#

Chicken Tikka Masala is a delicious and flavorful Indian dish, popular all over the world. It’s a favorite in many Indian restaurants, but you can easily make it at home with just a few ingredients. In this blog post, we’ll discuss the benefits of making Chicken Tikka Masala at home, provide a list of necessary ingredients, and offer step-by-step instructions for the preparation.

What is Chicken Tikka Masala?#

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular Indian dish made with marinated chicken, cooked in a creamy, tomato-based sauce. It’s usually served with rice or naan bread and is often considered one of the most popular Indian dishes.

Benefits of Making it at Home#

Making Chicken Tikka Masala at home has many benefits. First, you don’t have to worry about the quality of the ingredients used in restaurants, as you can choose high-quality ingredients for your homemade version. Second, you can customize the flavors of the dish to your liking. Third, you can save money by making it at home rather than eating out. Finally, it’s a fun and easy dish to make, so you can get the whole family involved in the process.

Ingredients and Preparation#

Necessary Ingredients#

To make your own Chicken Tikka Masala, you’ll need:

  • 1 lb. of boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon of garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions#

  1. Start by marinating the chicken. Combine the diced onion, garlic, ginger, garam masala, coriander, cumin, chili powder, and olive oil in a bowl. Add the chicken and mix until the chicken is evenly coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F. Place the chicken on a baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.

  3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  4. Add the heavy cream and the cooked chicken to the skillet. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  5. Season with salt and pepper, to taste. Serve over rice or with naan bread.

Conclusion#

Making Chicken Tikka Masala at home is easy and rewarding. With just a few simple ingredients and a little bit of time, you can make a delicious and flavorful dish that the whole family will enjoy. Here are a few final tips and tricks to make the process even easier:

Final Tips and Tricks#

  • Marinate the chicken for at least 30 minutes for the best flavor.
  • Add extra spices to the sauce, if desired.
  • Serve with rice or naan bread for a complete meal.
